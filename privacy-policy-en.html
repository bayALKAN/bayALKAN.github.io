Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2025
This Privacy Policy describes how MyNutrio app collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

Developer Information

Developer: Dayzen Studio (Individual developer)

Contact: info@dayzenstudio.com

App: MyNutrio - Nutrition and calorie tracking app

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Profile Data: Name, age, gender, height, weight (optional)
  • Nutrition Data: Foods you eat, calorie tracking, water intake
  • Activity Data: Step count, exercise information (from device sensors)
  • Goal Data: Weight goals, daily calorie targets, diet preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Which features are used, app usage duration
  • Error Reports: Technical information in case of app crashes (no personal data)

How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Provide personalized nutrition recommendations
  • Calculate calories and nutritional values
  • Help you track your progress
  • Improve app performance
  • Resolve technical issues
  • Send you notifications (with your permission)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All your personal data is stored locally on your phone
  • Your nutrition data is never sent to any servers
  • When you delete the app, all your data is permanently deleted

Security Measures

  • Data is stored encrypted
  • Security improvements are made with app updates
  • Third-party access is prevented

Advertising and Analytics

Google AdMob Ads

  • Google AdMob ads are shown in the free version
  • You can turn off personalized ads in settings
  • Ad preferences can be managed through Google Ad Settings
  • Data used for ads is subject to Google's privacy policy

Firebase Analytics (Anonymous)

  • Anonymous usage statistics are collected to monitor app performance
  • This data does not contain your personal identity
  • Only information about which features are used and error information is collected
  • You can turn off this feature in settings

Your Rights

Under GDPR and data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can learn what data is collected about you
  • Right to Rectification: You can correct incorrect information
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export your data (Premium feature)
  • Right to Object: You can object to data processing

Cookies and Local Storage

  • Local storage is used to save your app preferences
  • Web cookies are not used (mobile app)
  • Your preferences are stored securely on your device

Children's Privacy

  • MyNutrio is not intended for children under 13
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • If such a situation is detected, data is immediately deleted

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play Services: App updates and purchases
  • Google AdMob: Ad display (free version)
  • Firebase: Performance monitoring and error reporting

Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time:

  • Significant changes will be notified within the app
  • Updated date is indicated on this page
  • Continuing to use means you accept the updates

Data Deletion and Account Closure

To delete your data:

  1. Go to "Privacy Center" from app settings
  2. Use the "Delete All Data" option
  3. Or completely delete the app
  4. You can request data deletion by writing to info@dayzenstudio.com
